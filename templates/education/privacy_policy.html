Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how Ideal Classes handles and protects your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you apply for admission, create an account, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and academic details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for educational purposes, including processing admissions, providing course materials, and communicating important updates about your curriculum.

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To notify you about changes to our curriculum.
  • To provide student support.

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.
